---
description: Design system and UI/UX component creation
argument-hint: [--phase=design|implement] [--style=minimal|brutalist|corporate|playful]
---

Use the smite-aura skill to design design systems and UI/UX components.

**Phases:**
- `design` - Create design system specifications
- `implement` - Build UI components

**Styles:**
- `minimal` - Clean, minimalist design
- `brutalist` - Bold, raw aesthetic
- `corporate` - Professional business design
- `playful` - Creative, vibrant design

**Output:** Design tokens, component library, or Figma specs

**Usage:**
/smite:aura --phase=design --style=minimal
/smite:aura --phase=implement --component=Button
