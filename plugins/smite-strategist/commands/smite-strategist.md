Develop complete business strategy with market analysis, business modeling, and revenue optimization.

**Workflows:**

- `market-analysis` - Market size, competition, SWOT analysis
- `business-model` - Business Model Canvas, customer segments, revenue streams
- `pricing` - Pricing strategy, tiers, unit economics
- `personas` - User research, pain points, motivations
- `trends` - Market trends and opportunities
- `costs` - Cost structure and optimization
- `roi` - Return on investment and projections

**Output:**

- `docs/strategist-market-analysis.md`
- `docs/strategist-business-model.md`
- `docs/strategist-pricing.md`
- Financial projections and recommendations

**Usage:**
/smite-strategist --workflow=market-analysis
/smite-strategist --workflow=business-model
/smite-strategist --workflow=pricing
